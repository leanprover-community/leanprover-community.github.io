Edward Ayers (Jun 16 2019 at 21:25):

The basic problems with unicode accents are:
- they are rendered inconsistently depending on font and platform (eg sometimes the accent is hidden behind the character). Often they will be rendered in the wrong place (as evidenced by zulip).
- there are multiple sequences of unicode characters which look the same or very similar on the screen but which Lean will read differently.
- It can be finicky to get the editor to input diacritical marks correctly, for example vscode treats cÃå as two characters so you have to click right twice to go to the other side of it which is jarring, especially if you don't know how it was made. Also I notice in zulip editor that you can enter wierd states where the diacritical mark is bound to a space and so is rendered slightly before the character even though it looked fine in vscode ... etc
