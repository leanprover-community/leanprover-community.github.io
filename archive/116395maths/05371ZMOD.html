Mario Carneiro (Aug 01 2018 at 10:57):

Kenny Lau (Aug 01 2018 at 10:57):

theorem test : 449 ≡ -1 [ZMOD 5] :=
by unfold int.modeq; norm_num

Kenny Lau (Aug 01 2018 at 10:57):

181 ms
