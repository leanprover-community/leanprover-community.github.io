Chris Hughes (Feb 16 2020 at 16:08):

example {s : finset ℕ} (x : ℕ) (h : x ∈ s) : s \ {x} ⊂ s :=
finset.ssubset_iff.2 ⟨x, by simp,
  λ y hy, (mem_insert.1 hy).elim (λ hxy, hxy.symm ▸ h) (by simp {contextual := tt})⟩
