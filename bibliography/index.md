This is an incomplete collection of published papers that describe work based on mathlib or other
leanprover-community projects. We also provide a [.bib file](lean.bib) which collects Lean-related
papers. Please submit additions and updates via
[GitHub](https://github.com/leanprover-community/leanprover-community.github.io).

## 2019

* Jeremy Avigad, Mario Carneiro, and Simon Hudon. *Datatypes as quotients of polynomial functors.* ITP 2019.

* Mario Carneiro. [*Formalizing computability theory via partial recursive functions.*](https://arxiv.org/abs/1810.08380) ITP 2019.

* Sander R. Dahmen, Johannes Hölzl, and Robert Y. Lewis. [*Formalizing the solution to the cap set problem.*](https://lean-forward.github.io/e-g) ITP 2019.

* Jesse Michael Han and Floris van Doorn. [*A formalization of forcing and the consistency of the failure of the continuum hypothesis.*](https://flypitch.github.io/) ITP 2019.

* Robert Y. Lewis. [*A formal proof of Hensel's lemma over the p-adic integers.*](https://robertylewis.com/padics) CPP 2019.

* Minchao Wu and Rajeev Goré. *Verified decision procedures for modal logics.* ITP 2019.
